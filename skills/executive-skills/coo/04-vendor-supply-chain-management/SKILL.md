# Vendor & Supply Chain Management

**Persona:** COO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the COO | Procurement

## Description
This skill enables the COO to maintain a rigorous view of Wynn Resorts' strategic vendor relationships, supply chain resilience, and procurement risk exposure. It synthesizes vendor performance scorecards, active contract registers, and supply chain risk assessments to identify underperforming vendors, concentration risks, and contract renewal priorities. The skill is designed to support both the quarterly vendor review cadence and acute supply disruption response.

## When to Use
- When conducting the quarterly strategic vendor performance review
- When a critical vendor is underperforming against SLAs and a structured escalation or remediation plan is needed
- When a contract is approaching renewal and a position needs to be established on renegotiation priorities
- When a supply disruption (geopolitical, logistics, or financial distress of supplier) requires rapid risk assessment
- When the COO must present procurement risk exposure to the board or risk committee

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Vendor Performance Scorecard (Q4 2024) | Provides quantitative performance ratings across quality, on-time delivery, contract compliance, and issue counts for all strategic vendors |
| 2 | Critical Vendor Contract Register — Active Agreements | Maps contract terms, values, renewal dates, and risk tier classifications for all vendors with material spend exposure |
| 3 | Supply Chain Resilience & Risk Assessment — FY2025 | Identifies single-source dependencies, geographic concentration risks, and lead-time vulnerabilities across the supply chain |

## Output
A vendor and supply chain management briefing including: a performance league table of strategic vendors with intervention recommendations for underperformers, a contract renewal priority list with recommended negotiation positions, a supply chain risk heat map identifying the highest-priority single-source and geographic concentration risks, and a draft action plan for the top three supply chain risks requiring immediate mitigation. Includes escalation triggers and ownership assignments.

## Example Prompt
> "Before the Q1 vendor review, I need a clear picture: which of our top vendors underperformed in Q4, which contracts renew in the next 180 days, and where do we have single-source dependencies that represent material supply risk? Give me a prioritized action list."

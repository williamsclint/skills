# Change Management

**Persona:** COO
**Classification:** INTERNAL USE ONLY
**Department:** Office of the COO | Organizational Excellence

## Description
This skill enables the COO to plan, execute, and measure the people-side of organizational change initiatives at Wynn Resorts. It synthesizes employee engagement survey results, historical change initiative outcomes, and the standard change management playbook to diagnose change readiness, design adoption strategies, and predict resistance hotspots. The skill is grounded in Wynn Resorts' specific organizational culture and prior change history, rather than generic frameworks.

## When to Use
- When a major operational change (technology deployment, process redesign, restructuring) is being planned and stakeholder readiness must be assessed
- When employee engagement scores have declined and a structured response plan is required
- When a previous change initiative has stalled at adoption and course-correction is needed
- When the COO must present a change management plan to the CEO or board to secure investment
- When post-implementation reviews reveal that stated change goals were not achieved and root cause analysis is needed

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Employee Engagement Survey — FY2024 Results | Provides the current organizational health baseline, including engagement dimensions most at risk and employee sentiment drivers |
| 2 | Change Initiative Outcome Report (FY2022–FY2024) | Supplies historical change success rates, adoption patterns, and lessons learned from prior initiatives to calibrate current planning |
| 3 | Change Management Playbook — Standard Approach | Defines Wynn's approved methodology for change readiness, stakeholder mapping, communication, training, and sustainment |

## Output
A change management plan package including: an organizational readiness assessment based on engagement survey data and historical change patterns, a stakeholder map with influence and resistance risk ratings, a phased communication and training plan, a resistance management playbook for identified high-risk groups, and a 90-day adoption measurement framework. For post-implementation reviews, includes a lessons-learned synthesis with recommendations for the next initiative.

## Example Prompt
> "We're rolling out a new property management system across all hotel operations in Q3. Using our engagement survey results and what we know about how past tech rollouts went, assess our change readiness and build me a communication and adoption plan that avoids the mistakes we made with the last two failed implementations."

# Regulatory & Accounting Compliance

**Persona:** CFO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CFO | Internal Controls & Compliance

## Description
This skill enables the CFO to maintain a real-time view of Wynn Resorts' accounting compliance posture, including open audit findings, regulatory filing obligations, and adherence to applicable accounting standards (US GAAP, ASC 606, ASC 842). It synthesizes findings from internal and external audits, tracks remediation progress, and cross-references active policies to identify gaps. The skill is particularly useful ahead of board audit committee meetings and external auditor interactions.

## When to Use
- When preparing for an Audit Committee meeting and a compliance status summary is required
- When a new audit finding is issued and the CFO needs to assess overall exposure and prioritize remediation
- When external auditors request evidence of management oversight over financial reporting controls
- When a regulatory deadline is approaching and filing status must be confirmed across all jurisdictions
- When accounting policy needs to be updated in response to a new FASB or SEC standard

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Audit Findings & Management Responses Log (FY2024) | Identifies open control deficiencies, their risk ratings, remediation owners, and current status |
| 2 | Regulatory Filing Calendar & Compliance Status (FY2025) | Maps all mandatory regulatory and tax filings to deadlines and responsible parties to flag any at-risk items |
| 3 | Accounting Policy Manual — Selected Policies Excerpt | Provides the current authoritative accounting policy positions to assess whether practices are consistent with stated policies |

## Output
A compliance status briefing including: a prioritized list of open audit findings with RAG status, a filing calendar summary flagging any overdue or at-risk obligations, an assessment of policy-to-practice consistency for flagged areas, and draft talking points for the Audit Committee chair. Optionally includes a management response template for new findings.

## Example Prompt
> "I have an Audit Committee meeting in two weeks. Pull together the open findings from the FY2024 audit log, flag any regulatory deadlines in the next 90 days, and tell me if our current accounting practices around revenue recognition and casino markers are consistent with our stated policy manual."

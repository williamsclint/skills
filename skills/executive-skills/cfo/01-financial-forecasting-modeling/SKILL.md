# Financial Forecasting & Modeling

**Persona:** CFO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CFO | Financial Planning & Analysis

## Description
This skill enables the CFO to synthesize historical financial data, segment performance reports, and variance analyses into accurate forward-looking financial models and forecasts. It interprets multi-year P&L trends, identifies drivers of variance between forecast and actuals, and produces executive-ready projections with scenario overlays. Output is calibrated to Wynn Resorts' property-level and consolidated reporting structures.

## When to Use
- When preparing the annual budget or re-forecasting mid-year based on updated actuals
- When the board or investor community requires a credible financial outlook with scenario analysis
- When significant variances between forecast and actuals require structured root cause analysis
- When assessing the revenue impact of strategic decisions (e.g., new property openings, capital investments)
- When presenting multi-year financial trajectories to the CEO, Board Finance Committee, or rating agencies

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Historical P&L Statement (FY2022–FY2024) | Provides the multi-year actuals baseline from which trend analysis and forward projections are derived |
| 2 | Revenue by Business Segment Report (FY2024) | Supplies granular segment-level performance data including quarterly cadence and YoY growth rates |
| 3 | Forecast vs Actuals Variance Report (FY2024) | Reveals systematic forecast errors, root causes, and adjustments needed to improve model accuracy for FY2025 |

## Output
A structured financial forecast package including: a three-scenario (bear/base/bull) revenue and EBITDA model, segment-level growth assumptions with supporting rationale, key variance drivers explained, and a one-page CFO narrative suitable for board presentation. Delivered as an annotated model summary and a draft CFO commentary block.

## Example Prompt
> "Using the FY2022–2024 P&L, the FY2024 segment report, and the variance analysis, build me a base-case FY2025 forecast with a bear and bull scenario. Flag where our historical forecast accuracy has been weakest and adjust the model assumptions accordingly."

# Capital Allocation Judgment

**Persona:** CFO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CFO | Corporate Finance

## Description
This skill supports rigorous evaluation of capital investment proposals against Wynn Resorts' hurdle rates, strategic priorities, and portfolio balance requirements. It synthesizes investment business cases, historical ROI outcomes from prior capital decisions, and the current capital allocation policy to produce an objective, data-grounded recommendation. The skill is designed to cut through advocacy bias in capital proposals and surface the real risk-adjusted return picture.

## When to Use
- When evaluating a capital expenditure request above $5M submitted by a business unit
- When conducting an annual capital budget prioritization exercise across competing projects
- When a prior capital investment is underperforming and a stop/continue decision is needed
- When the CFO must present capital allocation recommendations to the Board Finance Committee
- When reviewing whether the company's hurdle rate or WACC spread assumptions need updating

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Capital Investment Proposal — Gaming Floor Expansion | Provides the specific investment case, projected returns, and stated strategic rationale to be evaluated |
| 2 | Investment Decision Log & ROI Outcomes (FY2021–FY2024) | Supplies historical base rates for projected vs actual IRR to calibrate whether current projections are credible |
| 3 | Capital Allocation Policy & Hurdle Rate Framework | Defines the minimum thresholds, approval authorities, and prioritization criteria against which the proposal is assessed |

## Output
A structured capital allocation recommendation memo including: IRR and NPV analysis with sensitivity table, comparison of projected returns against historical base rates, risk-adjusted scoring against the hurdle rate framework, a prioritization ranking if multiple projects are in scope, and a draft CFO recommendation for board or investment committee submission.

## Example Prompt
> "Evaluate the gaming floor modernization proposal against our capital allocation policy. Does it clear the hurdle rate? How does the projected IRR compare to similar past investments? Give me a recommendation I can take to the Board Finance Committee."

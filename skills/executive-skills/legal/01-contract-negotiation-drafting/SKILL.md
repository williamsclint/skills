# Contract Negotiation & Drafting

**Persona:** Head of Legal / CLO
**Classification:** PRIVILEGED & CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the General Counsel | Commercial Contracts

## Description
This skill supports the CLO and commercial legal team in negotiating and drafting contracts with maximum speed and positional discipline. It synthesizes the approved clause library, the technology vendor negotiation playbook, and the historical dispute and litigation record to identify the right negotiation positions, surface non-standard terms that require escalation, and draft contract language that reflects Wynn's approved fallback positions. The skill enforces legal and commercial red lines while enabling pragmatic deal closure.

## When to Use
- When negotiating a technology vendor, professional services, or hospitality supply agreement
- When reviewing a counterparty contract draft for deviations from Wynn's standard positions
- When a clause falls below the minimum acceptable position and escalation criteria must be assessed
- When a counterparty dispute history suggests certain contract provisions need strengthening
- When standardizing contract templates following a pattern of disputes or amendments

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Contract Template Library and Approved Clause Reference | Provides the standard, minimum fallback, and walk-away positions for all major clause types |
| 2 | Contract Negotiation Playbook - Technology Vendor Agreements | Supplies issue-by-issue negotiation positions, escalation triggers, and deal breakers for technology vendor contracts specifically |
| 3 | Contract Dispute and Litigation Summary (FY2022-FY2024) | Reveals which contract provisions have historically led to disputes, informing where to negotiate harder or add protective language |

## Output
A contract negotiation package including: a clause-by-clause redline assessment of the counterparty draft against Wynn's approved positions, a issues list ranked by risk and priority, proposed fallback language for each open point, identification of any walk-away conditions present in the counterparty draft, and a negotiation strategy memo for the lead attorney. For new contracts, includes a draft using approved template clauses.

## Example Prompt
> "The technology vendor has sent us their standard MSA for the new CRM platform. Review it against our clause library and the tech vendor playbook, flag every deviation from our preferred positions, and tell me which issues are deal-breakers vs negotiable. Give me a redline strategy memo."

# Cross-Functional Legal Counseling

**Persona:** Head of Legal / CLO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the General Counsel | Legal Operations

## Description
This skill enables the CLO to deploy the legal department as a high-value strategic partner to all business units, rather than a reactive approval function. It synthesizes the legal intake log, the product launch legal checklist, and the M&A legal playbook to ensure that legal review is embedded early in business decisions, that departmental demand patterns are understood and capacity is allocated accordingly, and that high-stakes corporate transactions have structured legal workstream management. The skill supports both operational legal counseling and major transaction management.

## When to Use
- When the annual legal department capacity plan is being built and intake volume by business unit must be analyzed
- When a new product, service, or commercial offering is being launched and a legal sign-off checklist must be completed
- When an M&A transaction is in early stages and the legal workstream structure must be established
- When a business unit is generating disproportionate legal intake volume and root cause must be understood
- When the CLO must report legal department performance metrics to the CEO or board

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Legal Department Intake Log (FY2024) | Provides demand volume, business unit distribution, matter type mix, and cycle time data to understand legal department workload patterns |
| 2 | New Product and Service Launch Legal Review Checklist | Supplies the structured legal review framework for product launches to ensure no regulatory or compliance area is overlooked |
| 3 | Mergers and Acquisitions Legal Playbook | Defines the legal workstream structure, outside counsel allocation, key deliverables, and timeline for M&A transactions |

## Output
A cross-functional legal counseling package including: a legal intake analysis by business unit with capacity implications, a completed or draft product launch legal checklist with sign-off status for each review area, an M&A legal workstream plan with staffing and timeline, and a legal department performance dashboard. For capacity planning, includes a recommended legal resource allocation model.

## Example Prompt
> "The Marketing and Casino Operations teams are launching a new high-roller loyalty program in Q2. Work through the product launch checklist for the program, flag any gaming regulation or data privacy issues we need to resolve before launch, and tell me if we have the internal capacity to handle it or if we need outside counsel."

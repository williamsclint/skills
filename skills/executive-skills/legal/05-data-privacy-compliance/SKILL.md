# Data Privacy & Compliance

**Persona:** Head of Legal / CLO
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the General Counsel | Privacy

## Description
This skill enables the CLO and Chief Privacy Officer to maintain Wynn Resorts' data privacy compliance posture across GDPR, CCPA, and the Nevada Privacy Act. It synthesizes the enterprise data inventory, DSAR log, and privacy impact assessment templates to identify compliance gaps, manage data subject rights obligations, and embed privacy-by-design into new products and technology initiatives. The skill is designed to keep Wynn ahead of regulatory requirements in a rapidly evolving privacy landscape.

## When to Use
- When a new technology initiative or product feature involves personal data collection and a Privacy Impact Assessment is required
- When a data subject access request is received and the response workflow must be managed within the statutory deadline
- When a data breach or privacy incident occurs and the regulatory notification obligation must be assessed
- When the annual data inventory review is scheduled and classification accuracy must be validated
- When a new jurisdiction's privacy law comes into effect and its applicability to Wynn's data practices must be assessed

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Enterprise Data Inventory and Classification Map | Provides a comprehensive map of personal data categories, collection points, retention periods, and applicable jurisdictions |
| 2 | Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) Log (FY2024) | Supplies DSAR volume, SLA compliance rate, and outcome patterns to identify process gaps and capacity requirements |
| 3 | Privacy Impact Assessment Template and Completed Example | Provides the structured framework for conducting PIAs on new initiatives, including risk scoring and DPO sign-off requirements |

## Output
A data privacy compliance package including: a privacy risk assessment for a new initiative (using the PIA framework), a DSAR status summary with SLA compliance rate and any at-risk open requests, a data inventory gap analysis identifying categories that lack legal basis or have exceeded retention periods, and a regulatory applicability assessment for relevant privacy laws. For incident response, includes a breach notification assessment with regulatory deadline mapping.

## Example Prompt
> "We're launching a new mobile loyalty app feature that tracks location data and purchasing behavior. Complete a Privacy Impact Assessment for the feature, tell me what data we can legally collect under CCPA and GDPR, what consents we need, and flag any design changes required before we can launch."

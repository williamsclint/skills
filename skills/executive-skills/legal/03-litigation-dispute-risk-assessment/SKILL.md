# Litigation & Dispute Risk Assessment

**Persona:** Head of Legal / CLO
**Classification:** PRIVILEGED & CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the General Counsel | Litigation

## Description
This skill enables the CLO to maintain a disciplined, financially grounded view of Wynn Resorts' litigation exposure, manage external counsel costs, and make sound settlement and reserve decisions. It synthesizes the active litigation register, external counsel rate cards and spend actuals, and the settlement authority matrix to produce a portfolio-level risk assessment, identify cases approaching settlement triggers, and ensure appropriate reserves are established. The skill supports the CLO in presenting litigation risk to the Audit Committee and board.

## When to Use
- When the quarterly litigation reserve review requires an updated probability-weighted exposure calculation
- When a new claim or threatened litigation arises and a preliminary risk assessment is needed
- When external counsel spend is running over budget and management action is required
- When a settlement authority threshold is approaching and approval chain must be followed
- When the Audit Committee or board requires a litigation risk summary for financial reporting purposes

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Active Litigation and Dispute Register | Provides current exposure estimates, probability of loss assessments, and reserve amounts for all active matters |
| 2 | Outside Counsel Rate Card and Legal Spend Report (FY2024) | Supplies spend actuals vs budget by firm and practice area to identify cost management opportunities |
| 3 | Litigation Settlement Authority Matrix | Defines the approval chain and notification requirements for settlement decisions at each dollar tier |

## Output
A litigation risk briefing including: a probability-weighted total exposure calculation by matter category, a reserve adequacy assessment, a list of matters approaching settlement authority thresholds with recommended next steps, an external counsel spend analysis with budget variance and cost management recommendations, and a board/Audit Committee-ready litigation summary. For individual high-exposure matters, includes a strategic assessment memo with options and recommendation.

## Example Prompt
> "I need to update the Audit Committee on our litigation reserve position. Calculate our total probability-weighted exposure from the active register, flag any matters where reserves look inadequate, identify which cases are approaching my settlement authority limits, and summarize our outside counsel spend vs budget."

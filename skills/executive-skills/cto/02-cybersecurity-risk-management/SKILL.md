# Cybersecurity Risk Management

**Persona:** CTO / Head of IT
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CTO | Information Security

## Description
This skill enables the CTO to maintain a comprehensive, board-ready view of Wynn Resorts cybersecurity risk posture. It synthesizes vulnerability assessment results, NIST CSF gap analysis, and the security incident log to produce a prioritized remediation roadmap, a control maturity trajectory, and executive-level risk communications. The skill translates technical security findings into business risk language for the CEO, CFO, board risk committee, and legal leadership.

## When to Use
- When preparing the quarterly cybersecurity update for the board or risk committee
- When a new vulnerability or CVE is disclosed that may affect Wynn production systems
- When a security incident has occurred and a post-incident risk assessment is required
- When the annual security budget is being justified and a risk-reduction ROI case must be made
- When a NIST CSF gap assessment has been completed and a remediation roadmap must be prioritized

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Vulnerability Assessment Report (Q1 2025) | Identifies active vulnerabilities by severity and system, with current remediation status and responsible owners |
| 2 | NIST CSF Gap Assessment (FY2025) | Maps current control maturity against target state across all five NIST CSF functions to identify highest-priority capability gaps |
| 3 | Information Security Incident Log (FY2024) | Provides historical incident data including root causes, detection methods, and business impact to identify systemic weaknesses |

## Output
A cybersecurity risk briefing package including: a current risk posture summary, a NIST CSF maturity heat map with top 5 priority gaps, a 12-month remediation roadmap with effort and cost estimates, an incident trend analysis with recurrence risk assessment, and a board-ready one-page cybersecurity status update.

## Example Prompt
> "I need to brief the Board Risk Committee on our cybersecurity posture next week. Pull together the vulnerability status, our NIST CSF gap priorities, and what FY2024 incidents tell us about systemic weaknesses. Translate it into a board-level risk summary with our top three remediation priorities."

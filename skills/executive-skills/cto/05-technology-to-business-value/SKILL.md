# Technology to Business Value

**Persona:** CTO / Head of IT
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CTO

## Description
This skill enables the CTO to clearly demonstrate the business value generated by technology investments and to articulate the technology roadmap in terms that resonate with non-technical executives and board members. It synthesizes the technology roadmap, historical ROI data, and board briefing materials to translate engineering and IT activity into revenue impact, cost savings, competitive advantage, and risk reduction narratives. The skill bridges the language gap between technical execution and C-suite accountability.

## When to Use
- When preparing the board technology committee briefing and business-impact framing is required
- When the CFO or CEO questions the ROI on technology spending and data-backed evidence is needed
- When the annual technology budget is being justified and each initiative must be tied to a strategic outcome
- When a major technology initiative has completed and a post-investment review must be conducted
- When the CTO is presenting the technology strategy to a new board member or institutional investor

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | FY2025 Technology Roadmap - Business Value Mapping | Maps each technology initiative to its strategic objective, expected business outcome, KPI impact, and investment amount |
| 2 | Technology Investment ROI Report (FY2022-FY2024) | Supplies historical data on projected vs actual ROI, revenue impact, and cost savings to validate the credibility of forward projections |
| 3 | Board Technology Committee Briefing (Q1 2025) | Provides the current board communication format and level of detail expected by the technology committee |

## Output
A technology-to-business-value communication package including: an initiative portfolio summary with business outcome and KPI linkage, a historical ROI validation analysis, a board-ready technology value narrative (one page), talking points for common CFO/CEO challenges to technology ROI, and a dashboard of technology KPIs with trend data. For investment reviews, includes a post-completion ROI assessment with variance analysis and lessons learned.

## Example Prompt
> "The CFO is pushing back on the FY2025 technology budget, saying our ROI track record doesn't justify the spend. Use the roadmap, the historical ROI report, and the board briefing format to build me a compelling, data-backed case for the technology investment portfolio that I can present at next month's board technology committee."

# Engineering Team Leadership

**Persona:** CTO / Head of IT
**Classification:** STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
**Department:** Office of the CTO | Engineering

## Description
This skill enables the CTO to make informed, data-driven decisions about engineering team composition, compensation competitiveness, and retention risk. It synthesizes headcount plans and skills gap analyses, compensation benchmarking data, and engineering attrition reports to identify where the team has critical capability gaps, where compensation is creating flight risk, and where engagement trends signal future attrition. The skill supports both proactive workforce planning and reactive retention interventions.

## When to Use
- When the annual engineering headcount plan and skills gap analysis is being finalized for budget submission
- When compensation benchmarking reveals material gaps to market that require a retention action plan
- When attrition in a critical engineering team is elevated and a root cause and response plan is required
- When a key engineer resignation triggers a broader retention risk review
- When the CTO must present engineering team health and talent strategy to the CEO or board

## Input Files Expected
| # | Document Name | Purpose |
|---|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Engineering Headcount Plan and Skills Gap Analysis (FY2025) | Identifies approved hiring targets, critical roles, priority skills, and gap severity by team |
| 2 | Engineering Compensation Benchmarking Report (2025) | Reveals compensation gaps to market at each level, enabling prioritized retention investment |
| 3 | Engineering Team Attrition and Engagement Analysis (FY2024) | Provides attrition trends, exit reasons, eNPS scores, and identifies specific engineers at retention risk |

## Output
An engineering talent briefing including: a skills gap priority matrix, a compensation action plan identifying levels and roles requiring immediate market-rate adjustments, a retention risk register for high-impact individuals, a recommended headcount allocation across teams, and a 90-day talent action plan. For board presentations, includes a one-page engineering team health summary with key metrics and forward outlook.

## Example Prompt
> "I need to finalize my FY2025 engineering hiring plan and figure out where our compensation gaps are creating the most retention risk. Cross-reference the headcount plan with the benchmarking data and the FY2024 attrition report, and tell me which teams I need to prioritize and which engineers I'm at risk of losing."
